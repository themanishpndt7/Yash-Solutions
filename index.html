FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

For optimal performance, we recommend servicing your water purifier every 6 months. However, the frequency may vary based on your water quality and usage:

  • Every 6 months: Standard service for normal water conditions
  • Every 3-4 months: If your water has high TDS (above 500 ppm) or sediment levels
  • Immediately: If you notice reduced water flow, unusual taste, or any error indicators

Regular maintenance ensures your purifier operates efficiently and provides safe drinking water. Consider our Annual Maintenance Contract for hassle-free scheduled services and priority support.

Our Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) is designed to keep your water purifier or chimney in optimal condition with comprehensive coverage. Here's what's included:

Service Benefits

  • Regular preventive maintenance (quarterly/6 months)
  • Free service visits (as per plan terms)
  • Filter cleaning and replacement (labor only, parts extra)
  • Complete system inspection and testing

Additional Perks

  • Priority customer support
  • Discounts on spare parts (10-20%)
  • Emergency service availability
  • Free water quality testing

Note: AMC plans are available in different tiers (Basic, Premium, Platinum) with varying levels of coverage. Contact us to find the perfect plan for your needs.

Watch for these signs that indicate it's time to replace your water purifier filters:

Reduced Water Flow

If water is trickling out slowly, your filters may be clogged.

Unusual Taste or Odor

If water tastes or smells different, your filters may be exhausted.

Time-Based Replacement

General guidelines (varies by model and usage):

  • Sediment Filter: 6-12 months
  • Carbon Filter: 6-12 months
  • RO Membrane: 1-2 years
  • UV Lamp: 1 year or 9000 hours

Important: Don't ignore filter replacement as it can affect water quality and damage your purifier. Contact us for professional filter replacement service.
